ABOUT

Marketing Community at Berkeley, or "MComm," is a UC Berkeley student organization whose mission is to provide undergraduates with the necessary resources to thrive in the marketing industry. Through our professional events and opportunities, we aim to enrich students’ understanding of the field while allowing them to form valuable connections along the way.

At its roots, MComm was originally the Berkeley Marketing Club, which was founded by the Undergraduate Marketing Association (UMA) in 2013. Since then, BMC has evolved into MComm to better meet the needs of those interested in marketing at UC Berkeley.


Education Professional Development Community
MComm strives to equip members with the marketing knowledge they need to succeed. We provide case studies and workshops and post daily informational articles on our Twitter page. Learn the fundamentals of the field, or build upon your foundation with us.
In addition to building your marketing strengths, MComm also offers a professional development opportunities dedicated to shaping you into the best candidate for any job. Attend our resume critiques, cover letter workshops, mock interviews, and more.
Where else will you find a group of talented, unique individuals dedicated to the world of marketing? Whether you are involved in a committee or are a general member, MComm will help you build a network of support for your future career aspirations.
