Marketing Community at Berkeley

ABOUT

Marketing Community at Berkeley, or "MComm," is a UC Berkeley student organization whose mission is to provide undergraduates with the necessary resources to thrive in the marketing industry. Through our professional events and opportunities, we are dedicated to enriching students' understanding of the field, while forming valuable connections along the way.

At its roots, MComm was formed by the Undergraduate Marketing Association (UMA) as Berkeley Marketing Club in 2013. Since then, BMC has evolved into MComm to better meet the needs of those interested in marketing at UC Berkeley.

Education

MComm strives to equip members with the marketing knowledge they need to succeed through case studies and workshops. Learn the fundamentals of the field, or build upon your foundation and challenge yourself to apply your education in future careers.

Professional Development

In addition to building your marketing strengths, MComm also offers a professional development curriculum dedicated to shaping you into the best candidate for any job. Attend resume critiques, cover letter workshops, mock interviews, and more.

Community

Where else will you find a group of talented, unique individuals dedicated to the world of marketing? Whether you are involved in a committee (such as Strategy or External) or come out to our social events, MComm will help you build a network of support for your future.
