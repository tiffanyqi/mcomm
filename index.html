Marketing Community at Berkeley

About

mComm is an UC Berkeley student organization that welcomes all undergraduates who are interested in the field of marketing.

We aim to provide resources, events, and opportunities that expose students to this industry. We hope to expand students' marketing education and build professional development skills so they can learn how to navigate the industry.​

In 2013, Berkeley Marketing Club was founded by the Undergraduate Marketing Association at UC Berkeley. The next year, BMC would become Marketing Community at Berkeley to connect with the next generation of marketing students.

Interested in getting hands-on experience in marketing? Check out the various committees at UMA!

Education

Members will learn the fundamentals of marketing and the tools to apply their marketing education in future careers.

Professional Development

Members can attend professional development workshops, where they will learn resume & cover letter writing, and other skills.
